FinSafe helps everyday Indian investors understand what financial YouTubers are actually saying — before you invest your money. We analyse any YouTube video and give you a clear, honest breakdown in simple language.

View Source Code on GitHub
The Problem
Millions of Indians invest based on YouTube advice — with no way to check if it's safe
India now has over 100 million active investors. Many of them rely on YouTube finance creators for investment ideas — but most of these creators are not regulated, not certified, and sometimes paid to promote certain stocks without telling you. Their videos can sound very convincing, but the risks are often hidden or mentioned only briefly. One wrong investment decision can cost you your savings.

FinSafe was built to protect you from that. Paste any YouTube video link, and we will analyse the entire video and show you exactly what the creator is recommending, what risks they didn't mention, and whether their advice can be trusted — all in plain, simple English.
How FinSafe protects you
When you paste a YouTube video link, FinSafe reads every word spoken in the video using AI. It then checks what the creator is recommending, looks up their past reputation, searches for related news, and gives you a clear report — including a trust score out of 100, a list of red flags, and a beginner-friendly explanation of everything in the video. You also get live stock charts showing how the recommended stocks have actually performed since the video was posted.
What FinSafe Does — 6 Features
FEATURE 01
Reads the Full Video
We extract every single word spoken in the video — nothing is skipped or shortened. This means our AI analyses the complete advice, not just a summary.
FEATURE 02
Trust & Accuracy Score
We give every video two scores out of 100 — one for how trustworthy the creator is, and one for how accurate their claims are. Based on AI research of their history and content.
FEATURE 03
Spots Hidden Risks
FinSafe automatically highlights things the creator didn't tell you — like hidden sponsorship deals, unrealistic return promises, missing risk warnings, and exaggerated claims.
FEATURE 04
Live Stock Charts
Every company mentioned in the video gets a real price chart. You can see how the stock actually performed from the day the video was posted until today — judge the advice yourself.
FEATURE 05
Beginner-Friendly Breakdown
Our Blind Sim mode rewrites the entire analysis in simple language — like explaining it to someone who has never invested before. Uses ₹ examples, zero jargon, honest tone.
FEATURE 06
Ask Questions About the Video
After the analysis, you can ask anything — "Is this stock safe for beginners?" or "What did they say about risk?" — and get answers based directly on what was actually said in the video.
India Markets Page
Live NSE & BSE news — updated in real time
The India Markets section shows you the latest financial news from the Indian stock market — covering 30+ major companies including Reliance, TCS, HDFC Bank, Infosys, Adani, and many more. News is pulled live, filtered for relevance, and sorted by newest first. The ticker at the top and the Market Movers section show you real price changes over the past two weeks — so you can see which stocks are actually going up or down right now.
